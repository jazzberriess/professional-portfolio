About Me

I'm an enthusiastic full-stack junior to the world of web development. For front-end development, I have skills in HTML5, CSS and JavaScript. I've also used libraries such as jQuery and CSS frameworks like Bootstrap and Skeleton. For back-end development, I have experience in node.js, express, a variety of npm's, SQL, MySQL databases and the Sequelize ORM. I've also used Insomnia for API testing and the Heroku platform to deploy completed projects on. Outside of web-specific skills, I have extensive experience in the customer service space, so my interpersonal skills and ability to work as part of a successful team are impeccible.

I'm currently expanding my skillset to include React.

I have two feline co-workers who sometimes like to help me with projects. They're both aspiring Koding Kitties, who seemingly want to specialise in keyboard accessibility.
